About Us

We are a small family business passionate about balloon artistry and event design.

Belinda Vasquez, CEO & Founder, and her daughter Valeria Mutevelli, Operations and Creative Director, both seek to bring memorable events through balloon artistry in their local Ventura County, Santa Barbara, and Los Angeles markets.

We are in the business of delivering joy! Through our creations, we create a fun and enjoyable atmosphere where our customers will cherish their unforgeable events.

Our specialty is in professional balloon design including beautiful organic garlands, custom balloon walls and specialty designs, and balloon arrangements using high-quality balloons.
